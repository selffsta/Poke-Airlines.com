THE FIRST POKEMON GAME!

The first Pokémon game, "Pokémon Red and Green" (released as "Pokémon Red and Blue" internationally), marked the beginning of a global phenomenon. Developed by Game Freak and published by Nintendo for the Game Boy, the games were first released in Japan in 1996. Players embarked on a journey as a Pokémon Trainer, tasked with capturing and training creatures while striving to become the Pokémon Champion.

MOST SELLING POKEMON GAME!

the best-selling Pokémon game is "Pokémon Red/Green/Blue," collectively known as "Pokémon Red and Blue" internationally. Released for the Game Boy in 1996 in Japan and in 1998 in North America, these games pioneered the Pokémon franchise. The success of Pokémon Red and Blue is evident in their widespread popularity, innovative gameplay, and the cultural phenomenon they ignited.
